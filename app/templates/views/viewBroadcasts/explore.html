How It Works

Zipline app works in 3 easy steps.

Broadcast Live

Broadcast your video live on Zipline. Show the audience your work you are doing or the message you want to share.

Connect With Your Audience

People can watch you live from anywhere (web or smartphone). You can interact with your audience live, send & receive messages.

Receive Funds

All our live broadcasts have the ability to receive money; it can be a donation, or fundraising. Money is deposited directly into your account.

Download Zipline App

Download free Ziplineapp from Apple & Google store today!

We allow anyone, anywhere to start broadcasting live with just their smartphone. Let the world know you are broadcasting live.
